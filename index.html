About Us

Because we thought we'd had enough of televised debates where people in their fifties shout at each other, and because the average age of podcast creators today is 37. We young people can finally speak out and debate issues that concern us. Every week, we invite a student from a different field (law, business, engineering, health, etc.) to our Great Assembly and we share our debates with you for a good half hour. A programme by students, for students.
